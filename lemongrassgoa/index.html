Lemongrass consists of Joy Dawson on guitar and voice, his sister Cecilia as our vocalist, and Anand Lobo on viola, mandolin, and voice. Both Anand and Joy fill in on lead or backing vocals when necessary, for some of the best three-part harmony you'll hear on the classic music you love. We have performed at Alila Diwa, BITS-Pilani Goa, the Oak Barrel in Panjim, and several weddings in North and South Goa.

Joy Dawson is self-taught on classical, fingerstyle, and pop guitar, and keeps his skills sharp by teaching guitar and music theory full-time.
Cecilia Dawson is an excellent chef and artist, and has a voice that fits perfectly for Lemongrass' acoustic style.

Anand Lobo plays the viola and the mandolin, and has used his education in electrical engineering to build some of the equipment that Lemongrass performs with.
He has built the microphone for his viola and mandolin, a custom triple-microphone stand and portable speaker for Lemongrass' three members to perform with, as well as sound-responsive LED lights that can be seen at all Lemongrass performances.

Our music ranges from genuine "old" oldies from the 1600s-1700s classical music (Bach, Mozart, Beethoven, Handel, etc), up to '70s and '80s classic rock (3 Doors Down, The Eagles, The Beatles, Eric Clapton, etc.), up to very recent top hits (Coldplay, The Weeknd, Eric Hutchinson, Hozier) - and quite a few jazzy / bluesy / instrumental numbers to spice things up.
