Hand-built 100W stereo speaker
with sound-reactive lighting

₹ 100000
₹ 90000 without lights

This speaker sports a Yamaha MG10XU mixer built into the rear panel, allowing it to amplify audio from up to 10 independent inputs at once. It can also be used as a stereo recording interface.

The most interesting distinction, however, is the LED strips built into front panel, which light up in direct response to any sound passing through the speaker. Click here to see the lights in action, and the technically-inclined can click here to see the Arduino code that makes them work.
